About HUKR

HUKR is a movement

HELP UKRAINE is an Ethereum based cryptocurrency project that not only collects but also generates donations by utilizing the power of DE-FI (decentralized finance) through what we call “Invest To Donate” to help Ukraine and its people. All collected and generated donations and their distribution are publicly viewable on the blockchain and are donated in their entirety (100%)

How we help

Crypto wallet

NFTs

Invest to donate

The idea behind the Invest To Donate model is to enable crypto investors to donate funds to charities whilst staying within their comfort zone of token trading.

The HUKR team does all the work of carefully vetting charities, collecting and processing the donation funds and every other step necessary to ensure the funds reach those in need whilst the investor simply does what he does best, invest & trade the HUKR token.

The majority of donations are currently processed through the California, USA, registered crypto charity platform Endaoment.org who then channels the donations to the following charities in equal proportions:

  • Revived Soldiers Ukraine
  • NOVA Ukraine
  • US-Ukraine Foundation
  • International Committee of the Red Cross
  • Hope for Ukraine
  • LELEKA Foundation
  • Ukraine Children‘s Aid Fund

All transactions made to Endaoment.org can be viewed on our dedicated donation tracker (Endaoment.org also provides a tracker where all transactions can be double checked - https://app.endaoment.org/ukraine)
