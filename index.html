    How we plan to help?

    • We utilize the ability to charge buy/sell tax via the Ethereum smart contracts and are thereby able to gather funds from you, the investors, and directly donate them to those in need whilst you do what you do best - invest & trade (investors don't have to send their money to unknown charities instead they invest, as they would normally, and we take care of getting the tax part of their investment to those that really need it). We provide proof of every donation via our web page and social media via Etherscan links. There is no team tax, all the money collected goes towards helping the people in Ukraine. If not avoidable, we use some of the funds to pay for marketing and exposure.
    • We will be creating NFT's from heartfelt imagery of the current Ukraine war situation and donate 100% of the proceeds.
    • We are also connecting with other projects in the space who are willing to support the cause and donate through $HELPUKRAINE. We already partnered up with multiple projects from the crypto space and have made joint donations.
    • We are currently looking into ways to pay for housing, rooms, AirBnb etc. for those people who had to flee from their homes.

    Invest to Donate

    Utilizing the ability to charge a transaction fee via Ethereum smart contracts, we use the fee proceeds to donate directly to support Ukraine during this time of hardship.

    Our donation pool is set up to donate directly to California based registered charity Endaoment.org who than channels the donations to the following charities:

    • Revived Soldiers Ukraine
    • NOVA Ukraine
    • US-Ukraine Foundation
    • International Committee of the Red Cross
    • Hope for Ukraine
    • LELEKA Foundation
    • Ukraine Children‘s Aid Fund

    100% of the donation pool is passed onto the charity. These donations are fully transparent on our donation tracker on the home page and the Endaoment website displays where the donations are being distributed via the ‘Grant’ page.

    6% Buy Donation, 8% Sell Donation – 100% Donated
