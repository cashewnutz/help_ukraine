    How we plan to help?

    • By offering people who are forced to leave Ukraine shelter in the neighboring European countries. We will connect people who are able to host and offer shelter with those in need of a roof over their heads. Whether you have a spare room, an AirBnb, an empty house or anything in between, those in need will be more than grateful and we will make sure they find their way to you.
    • By utilizing the ability to charge tax via the Ethereum smart contracts we are able to gather funds from you, our investors, and directly donate them to those in need.
    • By creating NFT's from heartfelt imagery of the current Ukraine war situation and donate the proceeds to charities the community votes on.
    • By connecting with other projects in the space who are willing to support the cause and donate through $HUKR. We will utilize the power of our joined communities to spread the word and raise as much awareness and funds as we can to support those in need.

    Our Taxes will be used to donate

    Utilizing the ability to charge tax via ethereum smart contracts, we will use our tax proceeds to donate directly to support Ukraine during this time of hardship.

    6% Buy Tax

    6% of all purchases will go directly to our treasury that will be used to donate directly to Ukraine. Part of this tax will also allow for marketing of our project and getting the word out about $HUKR.

    8% Sell Tax

    8% of all sell transactions will go directly to our treasury that is used to donate directly to Ukraine. Part of this tax will also allow for marketing of our project and getting the word out about $HUKR.

    20% Early Sell Tax

    16% of all early sell transactions (anyone who is selling before waiting 24hrs after their initial purchase) will go directly to our treasury that is used to donate directly to Ukraine and also utilized for marketing. 4% of all early sell transactions will go to strengthen the Liquidity Pool.
