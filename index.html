Our Taxes will be used to donate

Utilizing the ability to charge tax via ethereum smart contracts, we will use our tax proceeds to donate directly to support Ukraine during this time of hardship.

6% Buy Tax

6% of all purchases will go directly to our treasury that is used to donate directly to Ukraine.

8% Sell Tax

8% of all sell transactions will go directly to our treasury that is used to donate directly to Ukraine.

20% Early Sell Tax

16% of all early sell transactions (anyone who is selling before waiting 24hrs after their initial purchase) will go directly to our treasury that is used to donate directly to Ukraine. 4% of all early sell transactions will go the Liquidity Pool.
